ABOUT MIXMIXTV

Losing the essence of music, we started to create content that is faithful to music rather than stimulating content against the movement of large-scale and commercialization.

We form a music culture that allows us to share with more people in a new way out of limited places and time constraints.

We strongly believe that we are the curators of this culture; we highlight the artists who have shown their most recent activities and the artists who have shaped and led this culture in the past.

As of 2021, we have over 500 contents. We provide the range in real-time through YouTube live streaming, and after broadcasting, it is exposed to users of the above platform and continuously flows into the content.

"We value the experience that can be felt together through the textures, elements, and sounds of various dance music, centering on electronic music."
